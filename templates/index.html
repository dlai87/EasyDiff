Why MaxDiff?

MaxDiff (Best-Worst Scaling) reveals true preferences by forcing trade-offs

1

Eliminates Bias

Unlike rating scales, respondents can't rate everything high. They must choose.

2

Clear Rankings

Get a definitive ranked list of preferences, not ambiguous average scores.

3

Easy for Respondents

Simple best/worst choices are intuitive and require less cognitive effort.

How It Works

Three simple steps to actionable insights

1. Create Your Study

Add the items you want to compare. Features, products, messages - anything you want to prioritize.

2. Share & Collect

Send your unique survey link to respondents. They'll pick their best and worst from each set.

3. Analyze Results

See clear rankings with scores. Export to CSV. Make data-driven decisions.
